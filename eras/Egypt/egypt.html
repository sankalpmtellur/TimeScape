Ancient Egypt

Discover the mysteries of pyramids, pharaohs, and the Nile.

Key Highlights

  • Pyramids were tombs for powerful pharaohs.
  • Hieroglyphs were a sacred script with over 700 symbols.
  • The Nile River was central to agriculture and religion.

Cultural Achievements

The ancient Egyptians developed one of the earliest known calendars, advanced mathematics, and sophisticated engineering. Their architecture, especially the pyramids and temples, continues to awe the world. They were pioneers in medicine, preserving surgical techniques and healing texts on papyrus scrolls.

Belief System & Gods

Egyptians believed in a rich pantheon of gods. Ra was the sun god, Anubis the god of the afterlife, and Isis was revered as a mother goddess. The afterlife was central to their culture, leading to elaborate mummification rituals and tombs filled with treasures.
