Modern India

Journey through revolution, independence, and innovation.

Major Events

  • 1857: The First War of Indian Independence (Revolt of 1857).
  • 1947: India gains freedom from British rule.
  • 1950: India becomes a Republic with a Constitution.

Independence Leaders

Modern India's freedom struggle was driven by leaders like Mahatma Gandhi, Jawaharlal Nehru, Subhash Chandra Bose, Sardar Patel, and Bhagat Singh — each leaving a deep mark on the nation's destiny.

Scientific & Economic Rise

Post-independence India made rapid strides in science, education, technology, and economic growth, establishing institutions like ISRO, IITs, and the Green Revolution.
