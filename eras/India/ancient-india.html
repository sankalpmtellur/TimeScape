Ancient India

Unfold the wisdom of one of the world's oldest civilizations.

Key Highlights

  • Indus Valley Civilization - one of the earliest urban cultures.
  • The Vedas - ancient sacred texts forming the foundation of Hinduism.
  • Great rulers like Chandragupta Maurya and Emperor Ashoka.

Knowledge & Wisdom

Ancient Indians made groundbreaking contributions in mathematics (concept of zero), astronomy, yoga, and Ayurveda. Universities like Nalanda and Takshashila were global centers of learning.

Beliefs & Philosophy

The Indian worldview revolved around concepts of Dharma (righteous path), Karma (action), and Moksha (liberation). Philosophical schools like Vedanta, Buddhism, and Jainism shaped spiritual discourse.
