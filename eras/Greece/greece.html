Ancient Greece

Explore the roots of democracy, philosophy, and art.

Key Highlights

  • Birthplace of democracy in Athens.
  • Philosophers like Socrates, Plato, and Aristotle shaped thought.
  • Olympic Games originated in Olympia.

Architecture & Culture

Ancient Greece is renowned for its grand architecture—temples like the Parthenon, amphitheaters, and statues. Greek myths and epics like the *Iliad* and *Odyssey* became timeless literary treasures.

Philosophy & Legacy

Socrates questioned morality, Plato imagined ideal societies, and Aristotle laid the foundation for logic and science. Their ideas influenced Western civilization deeply, shaping ethics, politics, and education.
